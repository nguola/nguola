Giới Thiệu

Welcome to HyperTravel! I am an experienced and knowledgeable tour guide with a deep understanding of cultures. I graduated from Harvard University and have traveled to over 80 countries worldwide. My extensive experience comes not only from academic learning but also from firsthand experiences living and immersing myself in the cultures of the countries I've visited.
I have a keen ability to understand the psychology and behavior of travelers, allowing me to create unique and insightful tours. You will feel the warmth, enthusiasm, friendliness, and dedication in the tours that I lead. I will help you discover destinations in a genuine and meaningful way, offering you an authentic experience that you have never encountered before.
My tours are characterized by empathy, passion, and a genuine desire to share my knowledge and love for exploration. Whether you're a seasoned traveler or embarking on your first adventure, I will ensure that your journey is filled with memorable experiences and meaningful connections. Join me, and let's explore the world together in a way that goes beyond the surface and delves into the heart of each destination.
