ULTIMATE TRAVEL GUIDE 12 EXAMINATION MONTH PHA VIET NAM

Where and when to go is always a difficult problem for many people. Traveling like it the most is to go
when the weather is good or the natural scenery is beautiful. Below is a 12-month guide Travel to Vietnam for you to grasp and plan right away for 2022 with Hong Ngoc Ha Let's travel!

January travel

Da Lat cherry blossom season

The most ideal Vietnamese destination in January is Dalat cherry blossom. For Da Lat people, cherry blossoms are always in season The city's most beautiful flowers of the year. From the end of winter, the tree begins to lose all its leaves and expose its branches spindly and then suddenly blooms flowers, blooming brilliantly when spring comes. This is also the festival's purpose of traveling to Da Lat live virtually from the end of December until early January. At that time, the city was suddenly covered with dreamy pink color all the way, bringing Feeling fresh and full energy for a radiant spring.

Each moment of the day, cherry blossoms have a different beauty together. In the early morning, dew drops still linger on The flower petals give the bride a fragile, mysterious beauty. Until the sun comes up, Those flower petals returned with her true color of pink, fluttering in the wind, making many souls wonder The king could not bear to leave.

February travel

Ninh Thuan grape hunting season

In February, if you have the opportunity to travel to Ninh Thuan, you must definitely visit the lush green vineyards. At this time, Ninh Thuan vineyards begin to welcome a new color. That's the color of the tops Green shoots, purple grapes and golden rays of sunlight. Even better, this is also the time when grapes grow so strongly that when you come to the garden, you just... Seeing plump bunches of grapes hanging on the trellis looks extremely attractive and interesting. In particular, time March weather in this Vietnamese destination is not too sunny and there is no rain, so it is very suitable for you to visit Participate in outdoor activities.

Nha Trang sea snail season

If you are a fan of sea snail dishes, you should definitely visit Nha Trang once. Maybe, This coastal city is not the place with the most snail shops, but it is definitely the place with the most the most attractive snail genre.

Hunting snowfall in Sapa

Snowfall in a tropical country like Vietnam can be considered rare, but about 10 In recent years, it has snowed quite often in Sapa, which is why snow hunting is an activity Falling is very much awaited by people who love traveling. Successful snow hunting experience in Sapa is You must carefully monitor the weather forecast, especially the strong northeast monsoons.

Saigon pink trumpet flower season

The pink trumpet tree has been experimentally planted in the center of Saigon since 2009. The pink trumpet tree has a color similar to like cherry blossoms, bloom in April and last until June. Each year there are only a few days for people to Saigon can admire pink trumpet flowers blooming on the streets. Because the pink trumpet only blooms for 3-4 months one day it will quickly fall off.

March travel

Con Dao leaves fall season

The Con Dao almond tree is known as a symbol of the resilience of many generations of ancestors fell on hero island. Not only that, the almond tree also brings an economic benefit this place. That's why Con Dao people always refer to the almond tree with pride and affection.

Coffee flower season in the Central Highlands

People say that March in the Central Highlands is the most beautiful time of the year, the month of bees going to collect honey, the month of dating couples and the month of the coffee flower season blooming sweetly all over the world plateau.

Dien Bien ban flower season

Early March is the time when the flowers bloom the most. Ban flowers bloom white all over the back of the pass and mountain tops, especially on the streets of Dien Bien Phu city. Ban flower season lasts until the end of April and is often blooming associated with many festivals and games of ethnic minorities in the Northwest.

April travel

Hunting sea urchins in Ly Son

Nhum is available everywhere, but this is a typical specialty of Ly Son island. April, heaven The calm sea also marks the beginning of the sea urchin hunting season in this Vietnamese destination. At that time, if there is a chance to come Here you will have interesting experiences when you can catch strange sea urchins with your own hands.

Khau Vai love market (Ha Giang)

There is a market where buying and selling are not important, but warm and beautiful on the Ha Giang plateau. That is Khau Vai love market, also known as Phong Luu market, held once a year on the 27th day of the third lunar month. calendar. The unique market has contributed to helping the lives of people in this land become lively and preserved enjoy the unique culture here.

Hung King's Death Anniversary (Phu Tho)

Hung Temple Festival, also known as Hung King's Commemoration, is a sacred festival of the entire Vietnamese nation, to commemorate and express gratitude for the contributions of the Hung Kings, the first kings of the people, to the founding of the country According to legend, Lac Long Quan and Au Co are considered the Vietnamese ancestors, the parents of Hung Kings. The festival takes place on the 10th day of the third lunar month at Hung Temple, Viet Tri, Phu Tho. Weeks before, the festival took place with many folk cultural activities and ended on the 10th day of the third lunar month with the palanquin procession and incense offering at Thuong Temple

May travel

Ninh Binh's ripe golden rice

Ninh Binh has long been an ideal destination for tourists and professional photographers Karma. Especially in May, this Vietnamese destination enters the ripe rice season, the fields are in Tam Coc wears a proud yellow color, highlighting the greenery amidst the mountains message, creating a picturesque natural picture.

Ninh Thuan blue sea - white sand

Ninh Thuan, the "sun like sunshine, wind like phantom" area (Phan Rang), associated with sand, heat and gravel stone, still retains its pristine beauty, with typical Cham tower architecture, with blue sandy sea white, and many fruits typical of sunny and windy regions. Coming to Ninh Thuan, there are no tourists not only to admire the natural landscape, but also to enjoy countless local products with rich diversity, from products of the mountains, forests, seas, plains... and literary values Culture has been preserved for a long time.

June travel

Quy Nhon cool blue sea season

In the hot days of early June, it would be a mistake not to go to the beach and if you want to find one A sea area that has both beautiful beaches and delicious, cheap, diverse food... that's for sure Quy Nhon is definitely the right choice.

Bac Giang lychee season

The lychee season in Bac Giang starts around June and lasts until the end of July every year. Travel to Bac Giang. This season, visitors will definitely not be able to miss the opportunity to visit the famous "lychee kingdom". listen and enjoy this sweet fruit. The capital of this famous fruit is Luc Ngan district, a mountainous district located about 40km from Bac Giang city center northeast. Luc Ngan lychee has its own sweet, refreshing taste that cannot be found anywhere else

Sa Pa plum picking season

From late May to mid-June is the time when trees begin to bear sweet fruit. Travel to Sapa At this time, you will enjoy many attractive fruits of cold countries such as: plums, peaches, apricot trees,... most notably plums. Sapa plums are grown a lot in the area on the way to O Quy pass Lake, Ta Van or Ta Phin villages, check-in to these places, you can experience the feeling of picking yourself Ripe plums and enjoying them at the garden, this experience will surely leave you in awe excited.

July travel

The ripe Western fruit season

July is the time when many Vietnamese fruits are in harvest season, but talking about fruits is impossible Don't miss Can Tho in the West. Blessed by nature with fertile land and fresh water sources Abundant so the fruit in this Vietnamese destination is always loved throughout the country.

Go to Cao Bang to see Ban Gioc waterfall

With diversity and richness in natural resources and a culture rich in national identity, Cao Bang is one of the Vietnamese destinations that you should explore. Besides the landmarks history such as Lenin stream, Pac Bo cave, Cao Bang also owns the majestic Ban Gioc waterfall located in the middle of four sides. thousands of deep green forests and mountains.

August travel

Phan Thiet's dragon fruit season

When mentioning Phan Thiet tourism, many people immediately think of a land like a "little desert" full of Sun and wind. But when you arrive at this Vietnamese destination, you will see that there is much more to this place Other interesting things, one of which is the dragon fruit garden in Phan Thiet.

Hoi An is mild and cool

Prominent in the tourist route of the Central region, Hoi An ancient town is like a rustic, simple and poetic. Whether day or night, traveling to Hoi An still brings with it different attractive beauties making not only domestic tourists but even foreign tourists fascinated.

September travel

Dong Thap's lotus season has returned

Dong Thap Muoi lotus usually blooms during the flooding season from around July to October of the lunar calendar, so If you want to see lotus blooming, September is the most appropriate time. It can be said, lotus is a flower that is present everywhere in Dong Thap. From golden rice fields to fields covered with lotus in Thap Muoi, Tam Nong, Cao Lanh...

An Giang flood season

The annual flood season in the Mekong Delta originates from around September to November, when flood waters From the upstream, they massively flow downstream and then to the big sea, of which An Giang is one of the welcomes The flood comes the earliest.

Visit Yen Bai terraced fields

Mu Cang Chai (Yen Bai) is one of the most attractive destinations for you to consider and plan for fall trip. Mu Cang Chai has an enchanting charm in the gentle autumn weather Cool, shiny golden color spreads across thousands of hills and mountains.

Autumn flying leaves in Hue

What month does Thu Hue start? Maybe after the full moon of the seventh lunar month, "in the seventh month, the water jumped ashore" According to folk experience, it is also the end of the "summer retreat" season for monks in temples Hue origin. It is also possible that autumn will come right at the back-to-school season every year, when the streets of Hue are dizzy The colors of students' uniforms...

October travel

Go to Ta Xua to hunt clouds

Vietnam has many ideal rattan hunting spots, including Ta Xua. Point This trip to Vietnam is located in Bac Yen district, Son La province, Ta Xua has a Western beauty. The North is full of rolling mountains, steep and winding passes, and fog covers the road.

Watching the ripe rice season in the Northwest

If someone asks the best time to travel to the Northwest, it is definitely the ripe rice season at the beginning of October. At that time, this whole land seemed to be covered with a new coat of brilliant yellow shines the entire sky, creating a beautiful natural scene like a painting.

Ha Giang buckwheat season

Every year from October to December, especially November, the color is purple and pink. of buckwheat flower beds blooming again and spreading across the northern highlands. That's like an urge Many people make their way to Ha Giang to immerse themselves in the fragile beauty of this autumn flower.

November travel

I love Dalat so much! Wildflower season, pink grass hill and pink season hanging in the wind

During winter in Da Lat is when the weather is at its most beautiful, when the trees and flowers here are blooming, when Travelers enjoy the most when coming to Da Lat because of the fresh climate, cool breeze, and wild sunflowers everywhere In every corner of the streets, white daylilies bloomed everywhere and hydrangeas also bloomed shining in the fields. When it starts to stop raining, it also awakens Da Lat's wild sunflower season awake, calling forth the brilliant yellow color to light up the entire space of the dreamy plateau.

The land of yellow flowers and green grass, Phu Yen

Phu Yen is a peaceful land, where mountains, clouds, vast forests and seas easily captivate those who passionately immerse yourself in nature. In the movie that made a splash in theaters, “I see yellow flowers on the green grass" adapted from the story by Nguyen Nhat Anh, the beauty of Phu Yen appears many hearts fluttered. It is a winding railway close to Vung Ro beach, far away is Hon Nua Tao So the scenery is so beautiful. Those are fishing boats lined up one after another. Mountain scene Combined with the blue sea, it adds to the poetic nature of this land

December travel

Sapa and winter festivals

If you love winter and want to touch the snow once, then Sapa is definitely the place for you for you. Although on winter days, the scenery in Sapa does not have the same brilliant beauty as the season Spring and Summer, but the natural scenery is still majestic and filled with an incredibly mysterious color by thick layers of fog and white snow and ice covering the entire path.

Enjoy the beauty of Phu Quoc Pearl Island

December is the most beautiful time of the year in Phu Quoc. CNN once voted the Southern pearl island as An ideal destination in Asia for December alone. At this time, Phu Quoc is in the season of calm waves and calm seas. The clear blue sky is extremely pleasant... December on Phu Quoc island has many fun activities Play sea sports, dive to see coral... and many places to visit like home fish sauce barrels, myrtle gardens, pearl bases...
