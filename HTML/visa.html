What does a visa application include?

Whether you are applying for a tourist visa, to visit relatives, or to work, you must make sure your application includes the following: following documents:

- Personal profile:

  • Signed passport (valid for at least 06 months) and has a blank visa page.
  • 02 international standard 4x6 photos (photos taken no more than 6 months, bare head, white shirt, background white)
  • Visa application (form 48R) filled in completely and signed for confirmation (signature on the form). Declaration must match the signature in the Passport.
  • Notarized copy of all pages of the current household registration book with the visa applicant's name
  • Notarized copy of Birth Certificate
  • Original Curriculum Vitae certified by the local government where the visa applicant is posting living parasite (Translated into English)
  • Proof of single/married/divorced status
  • Detailed declaration of family background

- Financial records:

  • Notarized copy of personal account for the last 3 months
  • Confirmation of account balance with minimum value of 5,000 - 7,000 USD
  • Other financial documents (real estate books, vehicles,...)

- Job profile

+ Visa processing for the administrative sector karma:

  • Recruitment decision or appointment decision (in case of holding a position)
  • Application for leave to travel (Original Vietnamese and notarized translation into English – Or always do it in English)
  • Confirmation of work.

+ Visa processing for business sector under State management:

  • Recruitment decision, or appointment decision, or labor contract
  • Establishment license or Business Registration certificate of the Company where you work
  • Confirmation of work.

+ Visa for limited company

  • In case you are the person whose name is on the Business Registration Certificate, you only need to submit it Business registration certificate + most recent 3 months tax payment receipt
  • In case you are an employee: labor contract, work confirmation and registration Business registration of the company where you are working.
  • In case of retirees: Retirement decision or pension book
