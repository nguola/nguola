PROFILE INFORMATION INCLUDES

1/Passport valid for more than 6 months

2/Visa application (The visa application must clearly state the visa application date and must have a signature similar to the signature in passport)

3/Picture 4.5cm×4.5cm taken within the last 6 months

4/Guarantee letter from the inviter in Japan

- Work certificate of the sponsor (if it is a private enterprise, then the license business, or tax declaration)
- Income certificate of the guarantor:

(1) Tax payment slip (with income clearly stated)

(2) Tax payment declaration (must have stamp from Japanese tax authority)

(3) Income certificate (issued by a Japanese administrative agency)

- If you cannot present the above documents, you must present a photocopy of your savings book The item clearly states the account usage history within the most recent 6 months.
- Residence certificate of the sponsor. If you are a foreigner residing in Japan Foreigner's residence registration certificate (issued within the last 3 months)

* In case the visa applicant proves the financial ability to pay for the entire trip There is no need to present a guarantee. In this case, the following documents need to be presented:
- Bank account certificate in the name of the visa applicant
- Income certificate issued by a competent authority

5/Invitation letter (written in English or Japanese. Photocopies or faxes will not be accepted)

- This document is required, even if a guarantee is not required. The section stating the inviter must be right Clearly state your full name, address, phone number, and there must be a stamp after the name.
- Must clearly state all places visited in Japan and the content of activities. The example cannot be written in general terms such as "visiting relatives" but must clearly state the process leading to the protection wards, activities and places to visit

6/Residence program (no photocopy or fax) (see Japanese sample)

+ Attach confirmation of 2-way air ticket reservation
+ Must clearly state the date and time of arrival in Japan, return date and time, flight number, airport and activity program
+ Must clearly state residence address (address, phone number)
+ The stay program must be clearly stated day by day, however, in the case of the same continuous activities, in the date, month, year section it can be written: from day month year to day month year

7/Documents proving the purpose of entering Japan
- Documents proving relationship with relatives in Japan (birth certificate, civil status)
